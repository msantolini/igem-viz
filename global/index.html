Results from the iGEM TIES Project

Here are some data visualisations from the iGEM TIES project obtained during the summer of the 2020 competition. The project aims at collecting data through journaling and questionnaires to better understand how iGEM teams work, what makes them thrive and succeed in the competition. This year was very special, with the pandemic creating extraordinary conditions for such a study to take place. Here we show some insights into some of the data that was collected this year!
